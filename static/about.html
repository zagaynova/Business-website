About

Stamps are great fun. We love it.

On a sunny spring day of 2017 Kerem and Masha decided that the only thing the world needs is just another stamp website for people to come together, enjoy the stuff they like, buy stamps and admire those for ever and ever and ever. This is how Stamp Hub was born!

Your man, Kerem

When I was choosing a hobby, I heard that there was one called 'hobby of kings' because so many famous peps have been avid collectors. This is how I decided to collect stamps. I am very fanatic and would sell my mother if there is a stamp I really want to have. I am here for you to advise, educate, network and just share the love for the stamps.

Your girl, Masha

Stamps pfff... is there anything more boring than that in the whole wide world? Yes, coding! So this is why I'd rather collect and sell stamps. If you wanna talk about your stamp dreams, talk to me! I just adore people in this business and can't imagine my life without the delicious taste of the stamp's adhesive!
